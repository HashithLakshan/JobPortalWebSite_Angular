How to Hire A Your Photographer

Put your phone away and be fully present during your best moment. Learn how to book a photographer to help capture your next trip.

  • Inquire for Your Dates

    Fill out a quick form check availability for your travel dates with photographers in any of our catogories

  • Choose Your Photographer

    Within 24-48 hours, we will let you know which photographers are available for your dates.

  • Book Your Shoot

    Tell us your shoot preferences, such as ‘must have’ photos and preferred time of day. At this step you’ll pay, and your date is officially reserved with your photographer.

  • Plan the Details

    Collaborate directly with your photographer to finalize the start time and photo route. You will receive their phone number for any last minute changes.

  • Shoot Day!

    It’s all about having FUN during your vacation photo session! Enjoy roaming the city while we capture your travel story.

  • Love Your Wonderful Day Photos

    Within 5 working days, you will get a link to your private gallery with all the best shots from your shoot. Pick your favorite photos to download and the memories are yours to treasure forever. Photos are edited & high-res.
